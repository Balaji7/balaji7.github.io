    Frequently Asked Questions

    What services does the hospital offer?

    Our hospital offers a wide range of services, including emergency care, inpatient and outpatient treatments, surgery, diagnostics, maternity care, specialized departments (cardiology, orthopedics, neurology, etc.), rehabilitation, and more. For a detailed list of services, please visit our Services page.

    Does the hospital accept my insurance?

    Yes, we accept a wide range of health insurance providers. Please check with our billing department or visit our Insurance page for a list of accepted insurers. You can also contact us to verify whether your insurance is covered.

    Are there any visitor restrictions or guidelines?

    To ensure the safety and comfort of our patients, we have visitor guidelines. Please visit our Visitor Information page for specific visiting hours, visitor limits, and COVID-19 related protocols

    What are the hospital's visiting hours?

    Visiting hours are from 9.00AM to 2.00PM. Please note that some departments or patients may have specific restrictions. We kindly ask visitors to respect the visiting guidelines to ensure a quiet and healing environment for all patients.

    Do you offer maternity services?

    Yes, we offer comprehensive maternity services, including prenatal care, delivery, postnatal care, and special care for both mother and baby. We also provide classes and counseling for new parents.

    How can I contact a doctor?

    You can contact your doctor through the Patient Portal, by calling the hospital directly, or by requesting a call back through our website.
